Scoring Algorithm:
  - Keyword match: 0.3 weight
  - Co-occurrence: 0.5 weight
  - Priority bonus: 0.2 weight
  - Usage frequency: 0.1 weight

Confidence Levels:
  - High: score > 0.8
  - Medium: 0.5 < score ≤ 0.8
  - Low: score ≤ 0.5
